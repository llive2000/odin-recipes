Description

This classic Lasagna recipe layers rich, savory meat sauce with creamy ricotta cheese, melty mozzarella, and perfectly cooked noodles for a comforting dish that’s both hearty and flavorful. Ground beef is simmered with onions, garlic, and a blend of herbs, creating a robust sauce that’s the perfect base for the lasagna. Fresh ricotta cheese adds a smooth texture, while mozzarella brings that signature stretch and melt. The lasagna is baked to golden perfection, offering a satisfying meal for any occasion. Whether for a family dinner or a special gathering, this lasagna will surely be a crowd-pleaser. Enjoy with a side salad and garlic bread for a full, delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef with the chopped onion and minced garlic over medium heat until fully cooked.
  4. Add marinara sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the grated Parmesan cheese. Mix until smooth.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce, then add a layer of ricotta mixture, followed by a layer of meat sauce and a sprinkle of mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layering process until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake for an additional 25 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  10. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
